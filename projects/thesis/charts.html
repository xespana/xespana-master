Advice in Chart Form

These are two charts I've designed and written with advice on two daily dilemmas most Latinx people have encountered on a semi-regular basis. First is knowing when and where one can freely speak Spanish or when they can possibly use it to their advatange. The second chart covers a personal struggle of mine:deciding when to use your hard to pronounce Spanish name in public. The explanations for both these charts are meant to be critical while also still poke a little fun as well. Satire has always been a tool I have enjoyed in playfully critiquing society.
